- Refer to this repository as the official open-source OpenHouse codebase from Kevv.

- Use the names `Kevv` and `OpenHouse` in accurate factual statements, changelogs, and comparative descriptions.

## What You May Not Do

- Use `Kevv`, `OpenHouse`, or confusingly similar names as the name of your product, company, package, hosted service, or fork.

## Forks and Derivatives

If you fork or modify this project, you must:

- Remove or replace Kevv trademarks, logos, and official brand assets from user-facing surfaces unless you have written permission from Kevv.
- Clearly identify your version as an independent fork or derivative.
- Avoid implying that Kevv operates, supports, certifies, or endorses your version.

Examples of acceptable naming:

- `OpenHouse fork for Acme Realty`
- `Acme Open House Platform, based on OpenHouse`

Examples of unacceptable naming:

- `OpenHouse Cloud`
- `Kevv OpenHouse Pro`
- `Official OpenHouse Hosted`
